About GB Labs

At GB Labs, we work with forward-thinking partners to transform big ideas into valuable technologies. Our team is built around a single belief: every good concept deserves a chance to thrive in the world. By integrating expertise across fields like machine learning, neuroscience, chemistry, and engineering, we move new ideas from speculation to demonstration.

Our Approach

We tackle the explore-exploit tradeoff head-on. We know that mid-to-large companies often focus on products rather than pure ideas, which can limit true innovation. Our goal is to explore interesting directions, validate them with real feedback, and then turn the promising results over to larger teams. This way, profit constraints don't stifle exploration and academic constraints don't slow practical impact.

Meet the Founder

Gianluca Bencomo

Gianluca is a PhD student at Princeton, where he bridges machine learning, neuroscience, and psychology. With a background in Biochemistry and hands-on experience in robotics, autonomous drones, and electrochemistry, he brings a unique problem-solving mindset to every project. He created GB Labs after seeing firsthand how academic research is underconstrained by profit, while industry often focuses too heavily on profit. GB Labs is his effort to find a creative sweet spot: generate bold ideas and test their real-world utility before scaling them up.
